# Policy Brief — Verifiable Governance with the Adamantine Standard

**What’s new:** Treat official records as self-authenticating proof objects.
**Why now:** Misinformation risk + audit backlogs + public trust decline.
**What to do:** Adopt SOP v1.1, publish Certified Records with DOI + AVE IDs, publish verification notes.
**Metrics:** Reproducibility, verification time, accessibility.
**Pilot ask:** 10–20 docs/quarter; 90-minute onboarding.
