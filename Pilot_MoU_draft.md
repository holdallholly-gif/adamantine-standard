# Memorandum of Understanding — Adamantine Standard Pilot

## 1. Purpose
To pilot the Adamantine Standard for verifiable governance across selected high-salience documents.

## 2. Parties
- Maintainer (Independent Entrepreneur)
- Pilot Institution (Department/NGO/Oversight Body)

## 3. Scope
10–20 documents per quarter; PDF/A-2u outputs; DOI + AVE registration.

## 4. Deliverables
- Certified Records with DOI + AVE IDs
- Verification Note and public landing page
- Quarterly KPI report

## 5. Responsibilities
- **Maintainer:** tooling, SOP, training, verification support.
- **Pilot:** document selection, access to process, publication approvals.

## 6. KPIs
Reproducibility ≥ 99.5%; Time-to-verify ≤ 2 minutes; Accessibility ≥ 95%.

## 7. Data & Licensing
Narrative: CC BY-NC-ND 4.0. Scripts/Schemas: MIT/Apache-2.0. No personal data stored in ledgers.

## 8. Term & Exit
3–6 months, with handover of SOP and templates on completion.
